How to Use This Tool

1. Add or remove additional products in the "Manage Additional Products" section.
2. Fill out the client form, select products (all Managed Services are available), and specify quantities.
3. Click "Generate Roadmap" to create an AI-generated plan.
4. Review and download the PDF to include with your proposal.
